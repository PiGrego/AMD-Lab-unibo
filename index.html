About AMD Lab

We develop multifunctional composite materials with novel properties and applications. We investigate electrospinning techniques in many fields, producing nanodevices for composite materials, artificial muscles, piezoelectric sensors, filtering protection and more.

Our research involves designing and manufacturing composite structures, 3D printing, analytical modelling, finite element analysis, and material characterizations.
